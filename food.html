Food Safety & Nutrition Tips

Food Safety

  • • Store grains in clean, dry containers
  • • Check expiry dates before consumption
  • • Wash hands before handling food
  • • Keep cooked and raw foods separate

Balanced Nutrition

  • • Include vegetables and fruits in diet
  • • Consume adequate proteins and vitamins
  • • Drink plenty of clean water
  • • Limit processed and fried foods

Smart Shopping

  • • Buy from authorized PDS shops only
  • • Check quality of grains before buying
  • • Keep receipts for complaint purposes
  • • Report poor quality or shortage

Budget Management

  • • Plan monthly food budget wisely
  • • Utilize PDS benefits fully
  • • Buy seasonal vegetables for savings
  • • Avoid food wastage
